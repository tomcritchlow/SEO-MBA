The art of effective client management

For agency SEO professionals who want to do better work for bigger clients

The SEO MBA course on effective client management will help you:

  • Manage client expectations
  • Communicate more clearly and confidently
  • Get buy-in and budget for your recommendations
  • Retain and upsell clients more effectively
  • Advance in your agency career

Every successful project has a foundation of strong client management

Managing client expectations is all about understanding what they value. It's not extra work or additional - it's the whole game!

SEO is a complex discipline - we require other teams to allocate resources in service of our goals. We need various teams at the client's side to work on our initiatives, whether that's engineering, product, marketing, editorial or PR.

This course is designed for anyone who works at an agency and cares about building long term clients:

  • Consultants
  • Account Managers
  • Sales people

If you care about client success then this course is for you. After completing the course you will:

  • Understand how to manage client expectations
  • Deliver recommendations that actually get implemented
  • Close bigger, better clients
  • Upsell and retain clients for longer
