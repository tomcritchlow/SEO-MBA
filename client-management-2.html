Every successful project has a foundation of strong client management

Managing client expectations is all about understanding what clients value - putting ourselves in their shoes to understand the context for our project. It's crucial to understand the goals, resources and priorities that drive our clients so that we can provide recommendations and projects that create impact.

We want to deliver work that drives change, not documents that sit on a shelf and get dusty. And that requires building strong client relationships. Building client relationships is not additional or extra work - it's a crucial part of our project's success.

Client management matters because SEO is a complex discipline. We require our client's teams to allocate resources in service of our goals whether engineering, product, marketing, editorial or PR. We need to do more than provide recommendations, we need to advocate for SEO initiatives.

This course is designed for anyone who works at an agency and cares about client success:

  • Consultants
  • Account Managers
  • Sales people

After completing the course you will:

Manage client expectations more effectively
Communicate more clearly and confidently
Get buy-in and budget for your recommendations
Upsell and retain clients for longer
Be more skilled at building trusted relationships
