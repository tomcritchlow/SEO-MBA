Corporate Workshops

The SEO MBA course is designed for established SEO professionals who need to improve their business skills, become more confident dealing with senior executives and manage large SEO budgets.

Key outcomes:

  • Create a compelling SEO strategy and vision
  • Create a business model for investing in SEO projects
  • Create compelling executive-ready presentations
  • Become more effective at getting things done

If you're considering the course for your company and want to test drive it first, this option enables you to get a feel for the material. Option 3: Private Live Course

If you have a large group and want an immersive, live version of the course, customized to your needs then choose the private cohort.

The format is 5 weeks of 90-minute zoom calls where we go through the course materials together, with plenty of time for group discussion. The benefits of the live version are: guaranteed engagement, discussion and application to real challenges the group is facing and customized outcomes based on your goals.

Format: initial 60 minute conversation to align on goals and customize the course followed by 5 weeks of 90-min zoom calls. In addition, everyone in the course gets 1:1 feedback on the assignments.

Pricing: $12,000 (designed for 8-12 people)
