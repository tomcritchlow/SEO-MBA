Corporate Workshops

The SEO MBA courses are designed for individuals but scale well to teams. In fact some of the best learning outcomes are from teams who purchase a course as a group so that they can discuss course materials together and apply it to real work situations in front of them.

If you want to get in touch, book a chat via calendly or email me: tom@seomba.com

There are three standard ways for companies to engage for training and development:

Option 1: Test & Learn

If you're considering the course for your company and want to test drive it first, this option enables you to get a feel for the material.

  • Enroll 1-3 people in one of the courses
  • Each participant gets a private 45-min coaching session with Tom Critchlow
  • This price discounted from any future group purchases

Enroll 1-3 people in the course, with a bundled 45-min coaching session for each participant.

Option 2: Group Discounts

For large teams looking to enroll together, group pricing is available for courses as follows:

  • 3+ seats: 10% discount
  • 5+ seats: 15% discount

For 10+ seats get in touch to discuss custom discounts and solutions, as well as unlimited licenses for larger teams. The unlimited license allows you to enroll as many people as you want now and in the future for a fixed fee. This is appealing for companies that want to formally integrate the SEO MBA into their internal training program.

Option 3: Private Training

If you have a large group and want an immersive, live version of a course, customized to your needs then choose the private cohort.

  • Kickoff call to understand key learning challenges and goals
  • Customized course content based on your needs
  • A 90-min session once a week for 5 weeks
  • 1:1 feedback for everyone on the weekly assignments
  • Q&A time and group discussion of real work products
